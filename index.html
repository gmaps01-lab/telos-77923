Frequently Asked Questions About Boarding School

How much does it cost to enroll my child at Telos in Orem, UT?

This includes housing, meals, individualized therapy, academic instruction, and most activities. Some additional fees may apply for certain therapies or specialized services. Our admissions team can provide a detailed breakdown based on your child’s needs and answer questions about insurance reimbursement or financial assistance.
What is the typical length of stay for students at Telos?
Most students at Telos stay between 9 and 18 months, depending on their therapeutic goals and academic progress. The timeline is highly individualized—our team regularly reviews each student's progress and works closely with families to determine the best path forward. We prioritize long-term success, so transition planning is a core part of the process as students prepare to return home or move to their next placement.
Is Telos in Orem fully licensed and accredited?
Yes, Telos is fully licensed by the State of Utah as a Residential Treatment Center and accredited by Cognia (formerly AdvancED). Our clinical and academic staff are certified in their respective fields. We regularly undergo rigorous inspections to ensure we meet or exceed all state and industry standards for safety, therapeutics, and education.
What is the admissions process like at Telos?
The admissions process begins with an initial phone consultation to discuss your child’s needs and determine if Telos is a good fit. If appropriate, you’ll complete an application and submit relevant documentation. Next, our clinical team reviews the materials and may conduct additional assessments. Once accepted, we’ll coordinate with your family to schedule an enrollment date and provide a detailed onboarding plan, including travel logistics to our Orem campus. Our team guides families through every step, ensuring a smooth transition.
Does Telos guarantee therapeutic or academic outcomes for students?
While we are committed to providing the highest level of care and support, Telos cannot guarantee specific therapeutic or academic outcomes. Every student’s journey is unique, and progress depends on various individual factors. However, our evidence-based approach, experienced staff, and individualized plans give students the best possible environment for growth and healing. We work closely with families to set realistic goals and measure progress throughout the stay.
